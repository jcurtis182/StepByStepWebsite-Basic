You're moving to the Farm...

You've inherited your great grand-uncle's old farm plot in humble old FarmTown.
Armed with hand-me-down tools, crippling student debt, and a dream, you set out to begin your new life!

Grow and Sell Crops

With a dynamic time cycle, enjoy the passing of the seasons while also carefully planning which of the 50+ crop varients to harvest and when.

Meet the Neighbors

With over 25 fellow residents of FarmTown, there's plenty of room to be had for new friends, or perhaps even something more...

You've got a lot of work to do...

Starting a farm from scratch won’t be easy, but you're not one to back down from a challenge. Face down all obstacles and create the farm of your dreams!

And when times get tough, remember: this will always beat working in the service industry.

Better start plowing!

Upgrades, People, Upgrades!

Buy and evolve your old hand-me-downs into state-of-the-art dirt destroyers! There are 30+ tools to collect and upgrade, each customizable to suit your farmer's style!

What secrets do these barren plains hold?

Ever since The Dark Times™ came and wiped out all life, the old ways of living have all but disappeared from FarmTown. But you can practically smell the potential in the soil!

Or maybe that's just the manure.

Raise Animals

With a plethora of various farm life, there are nearly endless possibilities to earn money and make new friends!

The local cows will always have your back!
